Information

This is a new signup process for Wikimedia Developer accounts. If you experience any errors or issues signing up please contact the Infrastructure Foundations team at idm-help@wikimedia.org. Include as much information as you are comfortable with. Especially the time of day will be helpful for reviewing logs, remember to include your timezone.

By creating a Wikimedia Developer account and the related services, you agree to comply with the Terms of Use for Wikimedia Cloud Services and the Code of Conduct for Wikimedia technical spaces. In particular, you agree not to collect, store, or share private data or personally identifiable information, such as user names, passwords, or IP addresses from the individuals using your project, except when complying with the conditions listed in the Terms of Use.

Warning
Creating or using a Wikimedia developer account will publicly expose information to other users of Wikimedia Cloud Services: The email address you associate with your Wikimedia developer account will be publicly visible. When connecting to virtual machines in Wikimedia Cloud VPS using SSH, your IP address will be recorded and may be visible to other users of Cloud VPS or Toolforge.
